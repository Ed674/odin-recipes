Blueberry

Description recipe

Red, white, and blue American classic! Serve with ice cream, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup cold butter, diced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons instant tapioca
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 6 cups fresh blueberries
